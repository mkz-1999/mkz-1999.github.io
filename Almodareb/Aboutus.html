About us

About the Institute

Al-Modarrib Training Institute is an educational institute found in Riyadh city and many other cities in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to be one of the mechanisms and programs that care for training and qualification of Saudi youth of students, technicians, engineers and specialists in many fields within a modern scientific and technical perspective that cope with labor market requirements and meet competition and excellence challenges. The scope of work of the institute includes accredited qualification and development programs and diplomas. It provides the graduates of secondary schools, both scientific and literal sections, with training courses and workshops.

Our Vision

Our institutes seek to be one of the best training and developmental institutes Kingdom-wide through integration of theoretical and practical courses that the student may need in his job, in addition to opening the door wide for all to benefit from and get experience in all specializations available. The institute has qualified human resources with high scientific experiences at every place of our presence. We target qualification and upgrade of the trainee by giving him the latest developments and all what is new in his training courses within a practical training framework that include real world experiences.

Our Mission

To avail the chance of education and training to every citizen who has the desire to gain new skills and that by subordination of all human, educational and physical potentialities to meet the needs of the society by offering qualifying, training and professional variety programs that cope with the development requirements and plans and the market needs.

TRAINING - EMPLOYMENT

Al-Modarrib Training Institute provides you with its offer on the training program at all its branches around Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. In accordance with Saudi labor market, the establishments and companies seek to employ national manpower of a high degree of training and experience to upgrade its production efficiency and to attain the safe range that meets its labor size set by Saudi labor act and to get use of the awards and services offered to it within that range. We have worked on the training program that guarantee to your organization many privileges, of them:

Upgrade of organization’s production capacity through provision of high experience in both theoretical and practical fields.
Upkeep of the organization within the Top Green Range and to make use of the services given within that range.
On-job training that meets the organization’s needs and raise the level of qualification of its workers.

The Program Mechanism
The mechanism of the program works as follows
A contract shall be signed between the company and the institute through which the solicitation and supply of trainees will be done within standard time in collaboration with a national recruitment company, and to train the trainees within the agreed period of time and as per the company’s need to qualify its staff.
A contract shall be signed between the company and the trainees.
After signing the contract between the company and the trainee, the company can directly register the trainee with the General Organization of Social Insurance (GOSI).
By this, the company can maintain its safe range, make use of the privileges provided by the government to trainees and acquire highly qualified national staff.
